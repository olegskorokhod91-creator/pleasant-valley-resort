Plan Your Group Stay

Crafting the perfect architectural balance between coming together and retreating for privacy.

The Art of the Resort Layout

Unlike traditional hotels or singular large rentals, Pleasant Valley Resort is thoughtfully designed as a constellation of properties. We provide multiple independent units that surround central gathering spaces like our community center, fire pits, and the Barndominium.

This layout allows large groups, corporate retreats, and multi-generational families to vacation together without sacrificing personal space or privacy. You can gather for a massive barbecue or presentation by day, and retreat to your own quiet lodge room or cabin by night.

Popular Group Combinations

Family Reunions

House + Cabin 7 + Lodge Rooms

Perfect for families spanning multiple generations. The 3-Bedroom House serves as the main kitchen and gathering hub. Grandparents can stay in the accessible and quiet Lodge Rooms, while younger families take Cabin 7.

Corporate Off-Sites

Barndominium + Cabins 8-10

Utilize the massive Barndominium as your headquarters for team building, presentations, and game nights. Team members can take up residence in the surrounding private cabins, sleeping up to 18 people comfortably.

Destination Weddings

Full Resort Buyout

Reserve the entire property to house up to 40+ of your closest friends and family on-site. Enjoy an exclusive outdoor ceremony space, ultimate privacy, and control over the entire resort grounds.
